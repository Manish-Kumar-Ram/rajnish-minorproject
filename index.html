About Car Repair

What are the common signs that my car needs a repair?

▼

Common signs include unusual noises, warning lights on your dashboard, decreased performance, and leaking fluids. If you notice any of these issues, it's important to have your car checked by a professional mechanic.

How often should I have my car serviced?

▼

It's recommended to service your car every 6 months or every 5,000 to 7,500 miles, whichever comes first. Regular maintenance helps prevent major repairs and ensures your vehicle runs smoothly.

What should I do if my car overheats?

▼

If your car overheats, turn off the air conditioning, turn on the heater, and pull over safely. Wait for the engine to cool before opening the hood. Check the coolant level and add more if needed, but do not open the radiator cap while the engine is hot.
